About the Project

Fun United Dollar Cultura (FUSD) is a community-driven digital asset designed to explore decentralized value exchange and on-chain culture.

FUSD is not a stablecoin and does not represent or claim any fixed fiat peg. The project focuses on transparency, experimentation, and multi-chain accessibility.
